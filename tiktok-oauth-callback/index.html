OrderPally OAuth Service

Internal authentication callback service for TikTok integrations

About

This is a private internal service operated by OrderPally (a brand under 17LIVE Group) for handling OAuth 2.0 authorization callbacks from TikTok for Business APIs. The service securely receives authorization codes during the OAuth flow and is used exclusively for internal advertising performance reporting and analytics.

Endpoints

GET /api/advertiser-callback  — TikTok Marketing API OAuth callback
GET /api/account-callback  — TikTok Account Holder OAuth callback

Privacy & Security

This service does not store any user-identifiable data. Authorization codes are short-lived (≤ 10 minutes) and used immediately to exchange for access tokens via secure server-to-server communication. All data access is limited to advertiser accounts and TikTok accounts owned by OrderPally E-commerce.
